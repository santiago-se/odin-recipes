Pommes frites

The ultimate recipe! The only question: what's on the side?

Expense

135 Min.

Difficulty

Medium

Ingredients for 4 Persons

600g Potatoes (waxy variety)
Salt
750g shortening
Oil or clarified butter

Preparation

1 / 4

Wash the potatoes, peel them and cut them into rough sticks. Wash again in cold water so that the starch separates - this makes the fries hard. Dry pens well.

2 / 4

In a large pot (if you're a fan of deep frying, use the deep fryer) let plenty of fat get really hot. Test with a cube of bread â€“ it should sizzle immediately.

3 / 4

Important: Pre-fry the sticks in small portions for 2-3 minutes, remove them while they are still light yellow, and let them drain. If there are too many in the pot, the fat will cool quickly and be absorbed by the potatoes.

4 / 4

Throw the pre-fried fries into the grease bath a second time - this time almost all of them can go in together. Bake until crispy in 4-5 minutes, drain well, add salt and be amazed.
