Wiener Schnitzel

Don't bread the schnitzel until shortly before frying, otherwise the breadcrumbs will soften and the crust won't be as crispy.

Expense

25 Min.

Difficulty

Light

Ingredients for 4 Persons

4 Pork schnitzel (upper shell; approx. 150 g)
Salt
Pepper
2 Eggs(size M)
2 THE Milch
4 THE Flour
150g Breadcrumbs
100g clarified butter
4 wedges

Preparation

1 / 3

Pat the schnitzels dry, pound flat, season with salt and pepper. Prepare three deep plates. Whisk together the eggs and milk in the first, add the flour to the second and the breadcrumbs to the third.

2 / 3

First coat the schnitzels in the flour, then in the egg wash and breadcrumbs. Press the crumbs lightly.

3 / 3

Heat the clarified butter in a large pan and fry the schnitzels for 3-4 minutes on each side until golden brown. Degrease briefly on kitchen paper. Serve with 1 lemon wedge each.
