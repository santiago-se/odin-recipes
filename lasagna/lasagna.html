Classic Bolognese Lasagna

The classic lasagne comes from northern Italy, more precisely from the Emilia Romagna region, and is prepared with bechamel sauce, Bolognese ragout and fine parmesan. Our lasagne recipe also includes creamy mozzarella.

Expense

80 Min.

Difficulty

Medium

Ingredients for 4 Persons

1230 kcal pro Portion

200g Onions
2 Garlic cloves
3 Carrots
2 rods Celery
2 tbsp Olive Oil
600g mixed minced Meat
150ml Red Wine
1 Branch Rosemary
2 Bay Leaves
1 can Tomatoes (800g)
Salt
Pepper
70g Butter
60g Flour
800ml Milk
80g grated parmesan
freshly grated nutmeg
Butter for the mold
12 Lasagna pasta plates (without pre cooking)
200g Mozzarella
3 stems Sage

Preparation

Required utensils

Cutting board, kitchen knife, vegetable peeler, another large cooking pot, wooden spoon,
cooking pot, whisk, baking dish (approx. 25 x 25 cm), baking brush, 2 sauce ladles

1 / 17

200 g onions, 2 cloves of garlic

Cutting board, kitchen knife

For the Bolognese, peel and dice the onions. Also peel the garlic cloves and cut them into thin slices.

2 / 17

3 carrots, 2 sticks of celery

Cutting board, kitchen knife

Peel the carrots, wash and clean the celery, and dice both as small as possible.

3 / 17

2 tbsp olive oil

another large pot with lid, wooden spoon

Heat oil in a wide, large pot. Sauté garlic, onions, carrots and celery for 4 minutes.

4 / 17

600g mixed minced meat, 150 ml red wine

Add the mixed minced meat and fry for 3 minutes. Then pour in the red wine and reduce by half.

5 / 17

1 sprig of rosemary, 2bay leaves, 800 g tomatoes (canned)

Cutting board, kitchen knife

Rinse the sprig of rosemary and bay leaves, shake dry and add to the canned tomatoes.

6 / 17

Salt, pepper

Bring everything to the boil and simmer over medium heat for 10-15 minutes until thick and finally season with salt and pepper.

7 / 17

70 g butter, 60 g flour

Saucepan, whisk

Meanwhile, for the béchamel sauce, melt the butter in a saucepan and stir in the flour.

8 / 17

800 ml milk

Gradually pour in the milk, stirring vigorously with a whisk.

9 / 17

Bring to the boil and reduce to a thick sauce over low heat, stirring occasionally, for 10 minutes.

10 / 17

30 g grated Parmesan, salt, pepper

Stir in 30 g Parmesan, season with salt, pepper and nutmeg.

11 / 17

Preheat the oven to 180°. Remove the rosemary and bay leaves from the Bolognese sauce.

12 / 17

some butter for the mold

Baking dish, pastry brush, sauce ladle

Grease the baking dish with butter and spread some béchamel sauce on the bottom of the dish.

13 / 17

12 lasagna noodle sheets

Sauce ladles

Spread the pasta sheets, Bolognese and Béchamel on top in layers, ending with a layer of Béchamel.

14 / 17

2200g mozzarella

Tear the mozzarella into coarse pieces.

15 / 17

3 stalks of sage

Rinse the sage and shake it dry and pick off the leaves.

16 / 17

50 g grated Parmesan

Sprinkle 50 g of Parmesan over the lasagna, then top with mozzarella and sage.

17 / 17

Bake the lasagna in the oven (2nd rack from the bottom) for about 40 minutes until golden brown.
